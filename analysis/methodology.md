# Methodology

The opportunity model is intentionally explainable. It is designed for customer planning and BI prioritization, not black-box prediction.

## Opportunity Score Inputs

- ACV distribution gap versus category benchmark.
- Syndicated share gap versus competitor share.
- Fill-rate gap versus 96 percent service target.
- Promotion lift attainment and merchandising compliance.
- Category growth from syndicated-style data.
- Estimated margin upside from sales, margin rate, and gap severity.
- Confidence score that penalizes low fill rate, missing promotion history, and source uncertainty.

## Data Trust Inputs

- Source system.
- Check type.
- Severity.
- Pass, watch, or fail status.
- Freshness hours.
- Failed record count.
- Recommended fix owner.

## Reporting Package Logic

Each customer package selects the highest-ranked categories, calculates modeled margin upside, summarizes open stakeholder requests, and labels whether slides are ready or need source validation.
